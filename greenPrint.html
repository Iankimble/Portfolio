Green Print
A budgeting app that applies the 50/30/20 rule of financial planning.

Summary
Financial literacy is very important subject for me. I've seen many people in my life who have put themselves in very bad financial positions from poor financial decision making. Even worse, In some cases many people who I've encounterd would inherit poor habbits from the ones closet to them. The goal of greenprint was to create a simple budgeting tool that users could use on the go to generate a very quick and simple budget plan based off of the 50/30/20 rule of budgeting. Being able to visualize your money and how it is spent is the first step to financial literacy.

logo process
After much thought and experimentation I found that I could use the letter "G" and its circular shape as a pie graph. With some refinement and imagination I exaggerated the imaginative 3 quarters cirlce into the basis of the logo. Next, I used color to block of segments of the shape to visually represent the 3 saving categories of the 50/30/20 rule.
